The Normal Bread Company™

Selling normal bread since 1708

Our story is as beautiful as it is typical. One human man with a dream to bake and sell normal breads mixed flour and yeast. That man's name is Norman Bakes and he is our founder. As the years rolled on, Norman's dedication to creating high-quality, normal bread captured the hearts of the local community. Generations have since passed, but the essence of Norman's dream lives on in every loaf we bake. Today, The Normal Bread Company still uses the same traditional methods and ingredients, ensuring that each bite of our bread remains as "normal" and delicious as it was over three centuries ago. Our team, inspired by Norman’s passion, wakes up every morning with a singular mission: to continue bringing that perfect loaf of normal bread to your table. Visit us, and you’ll find not just a bakery, but a piece of history, where the past and present mingle in the warm scent of freshly baked bread. At The Normal Bread Company, we believe that simplicity is an art, and normalcy is extraordinary.
